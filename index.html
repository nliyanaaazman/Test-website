Who We Are

SELANGOR'S TRUSTED CONTRACTOR

Teamcoat Engineering Sdn. Bhd. has been a registered CIDB contractor since 1992, operating from our head office — Wisma Teamcoat — in Batu Caves, Selangor.

We specialise in building, civil engineering, and mechanical & electrical works, combining on-ground site expertise with rigorous quality management and full compliance to Malaysian construction standards.

As a G7-grade contractor, we are authorised to tender for government and private projects of unlimited contract value.
