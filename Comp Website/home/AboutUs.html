ABOUT THE COMPANY

Who We Are

Introduction

Teamcoat Engineering Sdn. Bhd was incorporated on 15 January 1992 with the objective of becoming one of the most significant players in the construction industry.

Registered with Pusat Khidmat Kontraktor (PKK) and CIDB in Grade G7, Teamcoat aims to respond positively to the need for more Bumiputera firms in the construction sector.

✓  MS ISO 9001:2015 (AR 3339)
🔧
Our Expertise

Nature of Business

Teamcoat has been actively involved in construction, waterproofing, specialist painting, floor coating, demolishing work, concrete repair, and structure strengthening.

Our company has attained CIDB SCORE 3 Star and is listed in the TERAS Programme under the Bumiputera Agenda Unit Leader (LEADING), Prime Minister's Department.

✓  CIDB SCORE 3 Star
🏛️
Track Record

Government Projects

Our direct and transparent involvement with JKR, DBKL, MARA, KKLW, MOH, MOE, RISDA, FELDA, INTAN, Parliament Building, Museum Shah Alam, and other agencies has given us deep experience and maturity in the industry.

Recognition and trust have been granted to our company as a Bumiputera firm selected to be more competitive, viable, and resilient in carrying out mega projects for both government and private sectors.

🎯
Our Vision

Commitment

As a high-esteemed Bumiputera firm, Teamcoat stresses the importance of high-valued services in the construction field — aiming to be one of the most successful contractors in Malaysia.

Teamcoat Engineering Sdn. Bhd is committed to becoming a leading Bumiputera Wibawa contractor and comprehensive service provider that delivers excellent construction and meets all statutory requirements.
